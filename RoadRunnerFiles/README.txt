This is the place where the generate_map.py will look for the maps.

The structure must be one folder per map, with the same name as the .fbx and .xodr files that will be inside said folder.

P.e.: 
RoadRunnerFiles/
├── AwesomeMap01
│   ├── Asphalt1_Diff.png
│   ├── Asphalt1_Norm.png
│   ├── Asphalt1_Spec.png
│   ├── Grass1_Diff.png
│   ├── Grass1_Norm.png
│   ├── Grass1_Spec.png
│   ├── LaneMarking1_Diff.png
│   ├── LaneMarking1_Norm.png
│   ├── LaneMarking1_Spec.png
│   ├── AwesomeMap01.fbx
│   └── AwesomeMap01.xodr
└── AwesomeMap02
    ├── Asphalt1_Diff.png
    ├── Asphalt1_Norm.png
    ├── Asphalt1_Spec.png
    ├── Concrete1_Diff.png
    ├── Concrete1_Norm.png
    ├── Concrete1_Spec.png
    ├── Grass1_Diff.png
    ├── Grass1_Norm.png
    ├── Grass1_Spec.png
    ├── LaneMarking1_Diff.png
    ├── LaneMarking1_Norm.png
    ├── LaneMarking1_Spec.png
    ├── AwesomeMap02.fbx
    └── AwesomeMap02.xodr
